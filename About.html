About me

Antonio Wenceslau de Freitas is an undergraduate student in Computer Science at Universidade Cruzeiro do Sul and in Computer Engineering at UNIVESP. A native of Ilhabela, São Paulo, Antonio currently serves as an intern at the Municipality of Ilhabela. He holds a degree in Financial Management and previously spent three years as an Administrative Coordinator for a healthcare company. Currently in a career transition, his academic and professional focus is dedicated to supporting and mentoring new students entering the technology field.
